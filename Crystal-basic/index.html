Crystal Collector!


You will be given a random number at the start of the game.

There are four crystals below. By click on each crystal youw will add a specific number of points to your total score.

You win the game by matching your total score with the random number, you will lose if your total score is above the random number.

The value of each crystal is hidden until you click on it.

When each game starts, the value of each crystal changes.
